Ingredients:
 - (OPTIONAL) sugar
 - 1 egg
 - 2 slices bread
 - ~1/4 c. milk
 - cinnamon
 - vanilla extract
 
Directions:
 1. Beat egg in bowl.  Mix in milk, cinnamon and vanilla extract (and optionally sugar).
 
 2. Dip slices of bread in batter, transferring to greased, heated pan ASAP.
 
 3. Cook, flip, cook, remove, serve with butter & syrup.
